Thats My Jam
OVERVIEW

"That's My Jam" music event app designed to connect music enthusiasts with live concerts, festivals, and events. The app provides users with a seamless experience to discover, attend, and engage with music events happening around them.

It serves as a one-stop platform for artists and fans, where user can bid live on their favourite song and the one with the higher bid will get chance to listen their favourite music played by artists.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Music fans struggle to listen their favorite music from the artist during the live event. Music enthusiasts often face challenges in discovering, accessing, and enjoying live concerts and music events. Traditional methods of event discovery and ticket purchasing can be fragmented, leading to missed opportunities and frustration for both fans and artists.

SOLUTION

User can now listen their favorite music by their favorite artist during the live event through the app by just bidding on their favorite song. They can easily search the events nearby their location. App provides robust search functionality with filters for date, location, genre, artist, and price range. They can see the artists who will perform in the event so they can plan accordingly.
